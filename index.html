Yasmin Epworth

Front End Web Developer


My name is Yasmin Epworth and I am 21 years old from Sheffield, England. I joined Manchester Metropolitan three years ago and began studying Digital Media and Communications.


Before I started my degree, I studied IT at Sheffield City College for two years which gave me a insight into the tech industry. My main career aspiration is to become a front end web developer for any major company that has a vast ratio of women to men in the tech industry. After realizing whilst in college that very few women were in the tech industry, it created a drive within myself to not give up due to diversity within the tech work place. Growing up within my household, I have always had to defend and develop myself as a woman independently as I have two older brothers who have always dictated my every move. By constantly having male authority in my life, it has encouraged me to become rebellious against the norms of society which I have implemented into my career aspirations.

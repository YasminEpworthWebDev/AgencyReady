My first website

This is the first ever professional-ish website I have ever coded. The website was apart of a 2nd year assingment. The website was based on myself and my interests. A lot of my interests surround Asian culture which is why I wanted to have a bright and bold banner as it reflects my personality.

Group Website

This website wasn’t created solemnly by myself. This website was created with my help and my team members help. This website was created in my 2nd year of University as a part of our coursework. We were instructed to create any type of website we wanted to but it had to be helpful for young adults/students who aren’t from Manchester or might be moving to Manchester for University. We decided to go with a burger theme website which helps navigate students to the best burger in their specific area (in central Manchester.) We included a home page, about us page, restaurants page, offers page and a contact page.
